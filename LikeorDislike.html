FORTNITE

Fortnite is one of those rare games that offers addictive gameplay,
well-designed world and a unique experience no other Battle Royale game has to offer,
not to mention being free to play. It is commonly regarded as a genuine cultural phenomenon and has amassed a player base of a whopping 125 million players,
a number which has been increasing rapidly since the last few months.
Despite all of this, the game has several flaws, which differ in each player’s eyes,
and if not fixed, will cause the player base to decrease rapidly.
However, when people say that the game is dying,
they mean that it is in a regressive state,
not like other games which have rapidly lost their player base in a matter of months.
